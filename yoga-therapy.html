Yoga Therapy

A whole-person approach to healing, balance, and embodied wellbeing.

I support women moving through grief, transition, pain, anxiety, and life's tender thresholds. Yoga therapy meets you exactly where you are.

The Approach

What to expect

My approach blends functional movement, breathwork, somatic awareness, nervous system regulation, and trauma-informed guidance to help you reconnect with your body, restore balance, and feel deeply supported.

Whether you're navigating a major life change, living with chronic discomfort, or simply longing to feel more grounded and at home in yourself, each session is personalized to your lived experience—emotionally, physically, and energetically.

Together, we may explore:

  • Functional movement based on your unique limitations and strengths
  • Breath practices to regulate your nervous system
  • Somatic tools for grounding, emotional processing, and resilience
  • Supportive dialogue that honors your story and lived history
  • Gentle home practices to help you integrate change between sessions

This is not a workout. It is a healing relationship—one that centers safety, compassion, accountability, and empowered transformation.

Is This for You?

Yoga therapy supports you through…

  • Grief, loss, or major life transitions
  • Chronic pain or fatigue
  • Anxiety, overwhelm, or dysregulation
  • Joint discomfort
  • Disconnection from your body or breath
  • A desire to feel more grounded, clear, and resilient

If you're unsure where to begin, I'm happy to guide you.

Sessions

The path forward

90 min

Initial Assessment

A full evaluation of your movement, posture, breath, and nervous system tendencies. We'll talk through your health history, symptoms, stressors, and goals, and I'll send you home with personalized somatic and breath practices.

60 min

Follow-Up Sessions

Progressive sessions that adapt to your needs week to week. Refinement of your practices, nervous system support, pain management techniques, and trauma-informed guidance.

20 min

Mini Check-Ins

Short virtual sessions for breath resets, emotional regulation support, and real-time guidance during moments of overwhelm. A bridge between your therapy sessions.

Private Yoga

Deep Release · Deep Rest · Deep Breath

Intentionally curated private yoga practices that support well-being—focused on improving mobility, deepening rest, or cultivating mindful breathing.

Yin

Deep Release

Long-held floor poses to unwind deep tension and improve mobility. Spacious, grounding, and deeply restorative for the fascia.

Restore

Deep Rest

Fully supported postures designed to calm the nervous system and invite deep rest. A reset for an exhausted body and mind.

Breath

Deep Breath

Guided breathwork and meditation to cultivate clarity, steadiness, and emotional balance. Simple practices you can take with you.
